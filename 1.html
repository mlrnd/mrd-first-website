System

Define

A system is a group of interacting or interrelated elements taht act according to a set of rules to form a unified whole.

A system, surrounded and influenced by its environment, is described by its boundaries, structure and purpose and expressed in its functioning.
Systems are the subjects of study of systems theory and other system sciences.
